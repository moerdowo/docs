### Overview

Mayar's Rating & Review feature helps sellers see and understand customer feedback on their products. This customizable feature allows sellers to easily configure the specific rating types and data they want to collect. You can set up different review options for various products, simplifying management and giving you more control.

### How to Manage Rating & Review?

Mayar system automatically collect rating and reviews for your product after customers buy the product, no need to manually collect them.

After collection you can follow these simple steps to manage a Rating & Review in Mayar:

1. Choose one of product on list.

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-13124715.png"
     alt="Screenshot 2025-08-13 124715.png"
     style={{ width:"40%" }}
   />
2. Select "View" from the product hyperlink.

   ![Screenshot2025 08 13124845 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13124845.png)
3. Select Rating & Review menu on List at right side menu bar.

   <img
     src="/images/ratingreview.png"
     alt="ratingreview.png"
     style={{ width:"44%" }}
   />
4. **Show/Hide Reviews:** This button toggles the visibility of customer product reviews.
5. **Select Reviews:** Use the checkboxes to select one or more reviews.

   ![Screenshot2025 08 13144154 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-13144154.png)

   <img
     src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-13145031.png"

6. You can display your product review on the product detail page.
