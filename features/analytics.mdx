### Overview

Analytics Page by Mayar offers a professional metrics dashboard out of the box. So you can stay focused on increasing revenue.

![anal.png](/images/anal.png)

## **Metrics**

### Total Transaction

Total amount of transaction for this Product.

### Total Transaction Nominal

Total product sales and subscription payments have been made.

### Payment Not Completed

Total product sales and subscription checkout left unpaid.

### Product Pages Viewed (Last 6 Months)

Toal product view since 6 months ago.

### Completed Checkout

Total product checkout that have been successfully completed.

### Checkout Pages Viewed (Last 6 Months)

Total view for the checkout page.

### Transaction Success / Paid

Total paid checkout.

### Active Visitors

Real-time Visitor tracker.

### Average Visits

Average of Visitor in a day.

### Average Duration

Average duration on checkout page.

### Today's Visit

Total visitor today.

### Visit this month

Total visitor this month.

### Visit this Year

Total visitor this year.
