## Overview

Mayar Webinar is a online-course class that enables you to create various Topic & Speaker more ease to access. Webinar can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Webinar

### Navigate to Webinar

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Webinar

### Create Webinar

- Click on the \+Produk dropdown button

  ![Screenshot 2025-08-18 163810.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-18163810.png)
- Click on the Webinar Link button

  ![Screenshot 2025-08-18 170320.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-18170320.png)
- Fill in the necessary details for the Webinar, with Date Month Year of Class that you have been set before.
- Payment Type: You can Provide free class & paid class
- Upload Image Cover: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image, likely for a promotional purpose.
- Note: Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- You can choose to create the Webinar in any Price that you have setup.
- Click on Buat Webinar.

### Create Pembicara - Webinar

- Click on the Tambah Pembicara Button
- Fill in the necessary details for the Webinar
- Upload Photo Profile: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image of speaker. Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- Click on Buat Pembicara

### Share Webinar

- Once the Webinar is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Webinar, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Webinar

Editing a Webinar allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Webinar: In the Webinar table, identify the Webinar that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Webinar.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Webinar. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Editing Pembicara - Webinar

- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Webinar

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Webinar by following these steps:

- Locate the Webinar: In the table, identify the Webinar and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Webinar, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Webinar Link

You get access to a default Webinar in the Mayar dashboard under Webinar. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Webinar

- Click on Webinar in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Webinar and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Webinar, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
