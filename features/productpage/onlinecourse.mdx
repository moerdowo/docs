## Overview

Mayar Online Course is a online-course class that enables you to create a structural class & course within various study program more ease to access. Online Course can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Online Course

### Navigate to Online Course

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard.
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Online Course.

### Create Online Course

- Click on the \+Product dropdown button

  ![Screenshot2025 08 18163810 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-18163810.png)
- Click on the Online Course Link button.

  ![Kelas Online.png](/images/KelasOnline.png)
- Fill in the necessary details for the Online Course, with Date Month Year of Class that you have been set before.
- Payment Type: You can Provide free class & paid class
- Upload Cover: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image, likely for a promotional purpose.
- Note: Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- You can choose to create the Online Course in any Price that you have setup.
- Click create button Online Course.

### Share Online Course

- Once the Online Course is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Online Course, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Online Course

Editing a Online Course allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Online Course: In the Online Course table, identify the Online Course that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Online Course.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Online Course. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Online Course

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Online Course by following these steps:

- Locate the Online Course: In the table, identify the Online Course and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Online Course, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Online Course Link

You get access to a default Online Course in the Mayar dashboard under Online Course. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Online Course

- Click on Online Course in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Online Course and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Online Course, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
