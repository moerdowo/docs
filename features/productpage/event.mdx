## Overview

Mayar Event & Acara is a online-course class that enables you to create a various interest event more ease to access. Event & Acara can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Event & Acara

### Navigate to Event & Acara

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Event & Acara

### Create Event & Acara

- Click on the Event & Acara Link button
- Fill in the necessary details for the Event & Acara, with Date Month Year of Class that you have been set before.
- Upload Image Cover: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image, likely for a promotional purpose.
- Note: Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- You can choose to create the Event & Acara in any Price that you have setup.
- Click on Buat Event & Acara.

### Create Event & Acara - Tiket

- Click on the Tambah Tiket Button
- Fill in the necessary details for the Ticket
- Payment Type: You can Provide free class & paid class
- Click on Buat Tiket

### Create Event & Acara - Pembicara

- Click on the Tambah Pembicara Button
- Fill in the necessary details for the Speaker
- Upload Photo Profile: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image of speaker. Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- Click on Buat Pembicara

### Share Event & Acara

- Once the Event & Acara is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Event & Acara, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Event & Acara

Editing a Event & Acara allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Event & Acara: In the Event & Acara table, identify the Event & Acara that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Event & Acara.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Event & Acara. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Editing Event & Acara - Tiket

- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm your modifications.

### Editing Event & Acara - Pembicara

- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Event & Acara

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Event & Acara by following these steps:

- Locate the Event & Acara: In the table, identify the Event & Acara and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Event & Acara, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Event & Acara Link

You get access to a default Event & Acara in the Mayar dashboard under Event & Acara. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Event & Acara

- Click on Event & Acara in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Event & Acara and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Event & Acara, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
