### Overview

Mayar Physical Product is a Comprehensive detail of Physical Product that enables you to accept payments on various Product Fisik with ease. Product Fisik can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Physical Product

### Navigate to Physical Product

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Physical Product

### Create Physical Product

- Click on the \+Product dropdown button

### Share Physical Product

- Once the Physical Product is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Physical Product, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Physical Product

Editing a Physical Product allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Physical Product: In the Physical Product table, identify the Physical Product that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Physical Product.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Physical Product. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Physical Product

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Physical Product by following these steps:

- Locate the Physical Product: In the table, identify the Physical Product and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Physical Product, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Physical Product Link

You get access to a default Physical Product in the Mayar dashboard under Physical Product. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Physical Product

- Click on Physical Product in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Physical Product and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Physical Product, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
