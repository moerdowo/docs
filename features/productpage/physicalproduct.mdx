### Overview

Mayar Product Fisik is a Comprehensive detail of Produk Fisik that enables you to accept payments on various Product Fisik with ease. Product Fisik can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Produk Fisik

### Navigate to Produk Fisik

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Produk Fisik

### Create Produk Fisik

- Click on the Produk Fisik Link button
- Fill in the necessary details for the Produk Fisik
- Category Product: You can chose one of category product including electronic product, beauty product, sport & etc.
- Upload Cover: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image, likely for a promotional purpose.
- Harga Grosir: Setup best offer price for purchase in large quantities for a wholesale.
- Note: Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- You can choose to create the Produk Fisik in any Price that you have setup.
- Click on Buat Produk Fisik.

### Share Produk Fisik

- Once the Produk Fisik is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Produk Fisik, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Produk Fisik

Editing a Produk Fisik allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Produk Fisik: In the Produk Fisik table, identify the Produk Fisik that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Produk Fisik.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Produk Fisik. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Produk Fisik

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Produk Fisik by following these steps:

- Locate the Produk Fisik: In the table, identify the Produk Fisik and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Produk Fisik, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Product Fisik Link

You get access to a default Produk Fisik in the Mayar dashboard under Produk Fisik. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Produk Fisik

- Click on Produk Fisik in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Produk Fisik and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Produk Fisik, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
