## Overview

Mayar Coaching & Mentoring is a online-course class that enables you to create 1 on 1 session within various topic more ease to access. Coaching & Mentoring with Expert Mentor can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Coaching & Mentoring

### Navigate to Coaching & Mentoring

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Coaching & Mentoring

### Share Coaching & Mentoring

- Once the Coaching & Mentoring is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Coaching & Mentoring, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Coaching & Mentoring

Editing a Coaching & Mentoring allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Coaching & Mentoring: In the Coaching & Mentoring table, identify the Coaching & Mentoring that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Coaching & Mentoring.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Coaching & Mentoring. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Coaching & Mentoring

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Coaching & Mentoring by following these steps:

- Locate the Coaching & Mentoring: In the table, identify the Coaching & Mentoring and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Coaching & Mentoring, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Coaching & Mentoring Link

You get access to a default Coaching & Mentoring in the Mayar dashboard under Coaching & Mentoring. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Coaching & Mentoring

- Click on Coaching & Mentoring in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating Deactivate Button and View Details Page

- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Coaching & Mentoring and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Coaching & Mentoring, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
