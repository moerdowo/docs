## Overview

Mayar Bootcamp is a online-course class that enables you to create a structural class & course within various study program more ease to access. Bootcamp can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

### Share Bootcamp

- Once the Bootcamp is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Bootcamp, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Bootcamp

Editing a Bootcamp allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Bootcamp: In the Bootcamp table, identify the Bootcamp that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Kelas Bootcamp Link.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Bootcamp. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Bootcamp

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Bootcamp by following these steps:

- Locate the Bootcamp: In the table, identify the Bootcamp and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Bootcamp, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Bootcamp Link

You get access to a default Bootcamp in the Mayar dashboard under Bootcamp. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Bootcamp

- Click on Bootcamp in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Bootcamp and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Bootcamp, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
