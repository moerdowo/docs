### Overview

Mayar Penggalangan Dana is a  detail of Fundraising that enables you to accept payments on various Fundraising with ease. Penggalangan Dana can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Penggalangan Dana

### Navigate to Penggalangan Dana

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Penggalangan Dana

### Create Penggalangan Dana

- Click on the \+Produk dropdown button

  ![Screenshot2025 08 18163810 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-18163810.png)
- Click on the Penggalangan Dana Link button

  ![Screenshot 2025-08-18 170320 - Copy (3).png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-18170320-Copy\(3\).png)
- Type Penggalangan Dana: You can chose one of Category Type for Fundraising.
- Fill in the necessary details for the Penggalangan Dana
- Upload Cover: The image is a placeholder for uploading a cover image, likely for a promotional purpose.
- Note: Leave the amount field empty for Optional field.
- You can choose to create the Penggalangan Dana in any Price that you have setup.
- Click on Buat Penggalangan Dana.

### Share Penggalangan Dana

- Once the Penggalangan Dana is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Penggalangan Dana, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Penggalangan Dana

Editing a Penggalangan Dana allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Penggalangan Dana: In the Penggalangan Dana table, identify the Penggalangan Dana that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Penggalangan Dana.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Penggalangan Dana. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Penggalangan Dana

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Penggalangan Dana by following these steps:

- Locate the Penggalangan Dana: In the table, identify the Penggalangan Dana and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Penggalangan Dana, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Penggalangan Dana Link

You get access to a default Penggalangan Dana in the Mayar dashboard under Penggalangan Dana. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Penggalangan Dana

- Click on Penggalangan Dana in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating QR Code, Deactivate Button, and View Details Page

- View QR Code: To access the QR code, you need to do checkout via VA or E-Wallet .
- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Penggalangan Dana and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Penggalangan Dana, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
