### Overview

Mayar Fundraising is a  detail of Fundraising that enables you to accept payments on various Fundraising with ease. Fundraising can be created using the Mayar Web Apps. This documentation focuses on creating and customizing payment links through the Mayar Dashboard.

## Creating a Fundraising

### Navigate to Fundraising

- Log in to your Mayar Dashboard
- From the Sidebar menu, click on Fundraising

### Share Fundraising

- Once the Fundraising is generated, you can copy/paste or share it directly with your customer.
- When the customer clicks the Fundraising, available payment methods linked to your Mayar account will be displayed.

### Editing Fundraising

Editing a Fundraising allows you to make changes to its details, ensuring that it remains up-to-date and accurate.

- Locate the Fundraising: In the Fundraising table, identify the Fundraising that you wish to edit.
- Access Edit Mode: Click on the associated icon, followed by "Edit." This action will open the editing interface for the selected Fundraising.
- Modify Payment Details: In the edit interface, you can make changes to various fields of the Fundraising. This includes updating the amount, expiry date, and more.
- Save Changes: After making the necessary edits, be sure to click the Save button to confirm and save your modifications.

### Viewing Payment Fundraising

You can access a comprehensive overview of each Fundraising by following these steps:

- Locate the Fundraising: In the table, identify the Fundraising and click on the associated icon, followed by View Details.
- Explore Insights: On the View Details page, you will discover a wealth of information, including payment link information, customer details, and payment history.
- Sharing: To share a Fundraising, click the "Share" button. You can copy the URL or directly share it on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Line & LinkedIn.
- QR Code Generation: Conveniently generate a QR code by clicking on the QR code icon. You can then copy it to the clipboard or download the image.

### Managing Fundraising Link

You get access to a default Fundraising in the Mayar dashboard under Fundraising. This default link, when shared to customers, will allow them to manually enter the amount they want to send/pay to you.

Below are the steps involved in managing the Default Fundraising

- Click on Fundraising in the left sidebar. You can copy the default link from here, which can then be directly shared with any customer. Additionally, you can add the link to any order-taking form or embed it as a button on your website.

### Locating Deactivate Button and View Details Page

- Unpublished Default Link: If you wish to temporarily disable the default Fundraising and prevent further access, simply click dropdown published button, followed by selecting “unpublished”. This can be valuable when you need to suspend the link temporarily.
- View Details Page: For a deeper understanding of your default Fundraising, select "view" hyperlink at every each product. This action will navigate you to the Details Page, where you can explore revenue insights and access a complete payment history.
