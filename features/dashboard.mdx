### Overview

Mayar Dashboard page is designed for online sellers, content creators, or digital entrepreneurs to monitor their business effortlessly.

Mayar Dashboard Page helps users:

- Track sales & revenue
- Monitor customer transactions and reviews
- Follow up on unpaid invoices
- Analyze sales performance
- Access common links (like payment or customer portal)
