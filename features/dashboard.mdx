### Overview

Mayar Dashboard page is designed for online sellers, content creators, or digital entrepreneurs to monitor their business effortlessly.

Mayar Dashboard Page helps users:

1. Track sales & revenue
2. Monitor customer transactions and reviews
3. Follow up on unpaid invoices
4. Analyze sales performance
5. Access common links (like payment or customer portal)
