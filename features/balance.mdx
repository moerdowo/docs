### Overview

The Balance Page on the Mayar app is designed to give users a complete overview of their balance and financial activities. It shows up-to-date information about account balance, recent transactions, and visual expense analysis, making it easier to manage finances effectively. In addition, the Balance Page provides budgeting tools and sends notifications for important transactions, helping users stay informed and in control of their finances. With its intuitive and user-friendly interface, the Balance Page is an essential tool for Mayar users to achieve their financial goals.

### How to Withdraw Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Mayar Dashboard">
    Make sure you are logged into your account and on the main Dashboard page. This is where you’ll start the withdrawal process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the “Balance” Page">
    On the right sidebar of the Dashboard, find and click **“Balance.”** You’ll see two options: **“Withdraw & Top Up”** and **“History.”** Since you want to withdraw funds, select **“Withdraw & Top Up.”**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make a Withdrawal">
    Ensure you’ve completed verification and linked your bank account beforehand. Enter the amount you want to withdraw in the **“Withdraw Balance”** field (note: the maximum withdrawal limit on Mayar is IDR 250 million). Once entered, click **“Withdraw Balance.”**

    ![Gambar62 Pn](/images/gambar62.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Withdrawal">
    A confirmation modal will appear, showing details such as amount, fee, bank/E-Wallet name, account number, and account holder name. If all details are correct, you’ll be asked to enter a confirmation code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Successful Withdrawal">
    You’ll receive a confirmation code via email. Enter the code to complete the process. You can track the status of your withdrawal in the **“Withdrawal History”** tab. Once the status changes to **“Paid,”** congratulations — your withdrawal is complete\!
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to Top Up Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Mayar Dashboard">
    Make sure you are logged into your account and on the main Dashboard page. This is where you’ll start the top-up process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the “Balance” Page">
    On the right sidebar of the Dashboard, click **“Balance.”** You’ll see two options: **“Withdraw & Top Up”** and **“History.”** Since you want to add funds, select **“Withdraw & Top Up.”**

    ![Gambar64 Pn](/images/gambar64.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare for Top Up">
    Before topping up, make sure your account is verified and your bank account is set up. Once ready, click the **“Top Up”** button located next to your total balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process the Top Up">
    A **“Top Up”** window will appear, where you can choose your payment method (e.g., BNI, Mandiri, BRI, Permata Bank, or other supported banks). Enter the amount you want to top up and complete the payment using your chosen bank. Once the payment is successful, the transaction will appear in **“History,”** and your balance will be updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>
