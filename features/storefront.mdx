### Overview

The Storefront feature in Mayar is an innovative solution designed to help business owners quickly and efficiently create their own online store. With Storefront, users can showcase their products attractively, manage inventory, and securely process payments—all within one integrated platform. It also offers various marketing tools to help boost product visibility and reach more customers. The user-friendly interface ensures that even those without technical skills can easily manage their store, making Storefront an ideal choice for businesses of all sizes looking to expand their digital market reach.

### **Types of Homepage Layouts in MAYAR**

MAYAR offers 7 types of homepage layouts, each with its own unique function. Here’s a breakdown of each type:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Homepage – Link Bio + Catalog (Default)">
    This layout combines a link-in-bio feature with a product catalog. Users can display commonly used social media links alongside their product catalog, allowing visitors to access important links while browsing available products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Homepage – PayMe">
    This layout focuses on the payment feature. PayMe makes it easy for users to receive payments by displaying a simple and efficient checkout page.

    ![Gambar HD64 Pn](/images/gambarHD64.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Homepage – Product Catalog">
    This layout showcases a complete product catalog, including images, descriptions, and prices. Perfect for businesses that want to highlight their products. Users can also customize the display by adding announcements, choosing featured products, enabling an alternative header, and adding a slider.

    ![Gambar39 Pn](/images/gambar39.png)

    ![download.png](/images/download.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Homepage – Donation V1">
    The first version of the donation page is designed to make fundraising easier, showing information about the donation purpose, target amount, and how to contribute.

    ![Gambar HD67 Pn](/images/gambarHD67.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Homepage – Donation V2">
    The second version adds more features to the first one, such as a more user-friendly interface and more flexible donation options.

    ![Gambar HD68 Pn](/images/gambarHD68.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Homepage – Generic Donation">
    A simpler version of the donation page, designed for general fundraising campaigns without special features, offering a checkout display and a short description.

    ![Gambar HD69 Pn](/images/gambarHD69.png)

  <Step title="Homepage – Landing Page">
    A landing page is designed to welcome visitors from marketing campaigns or advertisements, with specific goals such as collecting contact information or promoting a specific product.
