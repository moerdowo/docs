## Request Body

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>
  Unique identifier of the webinar product to edit. This is read from the request
  body.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="name" type="string">
  Webinar product name.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="description" type="string">
  Webinar product description.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="amount" type="integer">
  Webinar price amount.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="crossoutPrice" type="integer">
  Crossed-out price displayed as the original price.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="eventUrl" type="string">
  Webinar URL, such as Zoom or meeting link.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="redirectUrl" type="string">
  URL where the customer will be redirected after completing payment.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="expiredAt" type="string | datetime">
  Webinar product expiration date in ISO 8601 format.
</ParamField>
